### Your ITSM Team's Invisible Cost

Every day, your service desk and IT operations teams handle hundreds of tickets that follow predictable patterns — access requests, password resets, permission changes, ticket routing, escalation handling. Each ticket touches 2–4 systems. Each one takes 10–30 minutes of manual work.

**The maths:**
- 500 repetitive tickets/month × 20 min average = 167 hours/month of manual cross-system work
- At EUR 35/hour fully loaded = EUR 70,000/year in operational cost for one workflow category
- Plus: 3–8% error rate, SLA breaches, quarterly audit prep consuming 100+ hours

This work is invisible in most ITSM dashboards because it's spread across dozens of people doing 5-minute tasks all day.

### What Ant Automations Does for ITSM Teams

We deploy AI workers that handle the repetitive cross-system work your team does between ServiceNow (or Jira SM / Zendesk) and your identity, infrastructure, and business systems.

**How it works:**

| Phase | What Happens | Your Control |

| **Propose** | AI proposes each action for the ticket before executing | Your team approves every step |
