## Outreach Guidelines

### Personalisation Requirements

Before sending any outreach:
1. **Confirm tech stack** — Check job postings, LinkedIn profiles, and company website for ServiceNow/Jira SM/Zendesk and Entra ID/Okta signals
2. **Identify pain signals** — Hiring for IT ops roles (capacity pressure), compliance certifications (audit burden), recent ITSM migration (process disruption)
3. **Localise language** — Use DE for DACH prospects, EN for Benelux. Templates above are EN; translate and adapt for DE outreach
4. **Reference specifics** — Mention their ITSM platform by name, reference their industry's compliance requirements

### Sending Cadence

- **Email:** 3-touch sequence over 10 days. No more than 1 follow-up per week after sequence ends.
- **LinkedIn:** Connection request + 2 messages over 5 days. No further messages if no response.
- **Combined:** If using both channels, stagger — LinkedIn first, email 2 days later.

### Response Handling

| Response Type | Action |
|---|---|
| Positive / interested | Schedule 20-min discovery call within 48 hours |
| Questions about product | Send one-pager + offer demo call |
| "Not now" / timing issue | Ask when to follow up, set reminder |
| Referral to colleague | Thank them, reach out to referral with their name as intro |
| No response after full sequence | Add to quarterly newsletter list, re-engage in 3 months |
| Unsubscribe / not interested | Remove from all outreach immediately |

### Compliance Notes

- All outreach must comply with GDPR (legitimate interest basis for B2B outreach)
- Include unsubscribe option in all emails
- Respect opt-out requests immediately
- Do not purchase email lists — use LinkedIn Sales Navigator, company websites, and conference attendee lists only
